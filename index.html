The Crisis

What's happening
beneath the surface

Overfishing

Industrial fleets strip fish stocks faster than they can recover. Keystone species collapse, threatening food security for over 600 million people worldwide.

1 in 3 stocks overexploited

Pollution

From the Great Pacific Garbage Patch to microplastics in the deepest trenches, human waste has infiltrated every marine ecosystem on Earth.

51 trillion microplastic particles

Climate Change

Oceans absorb 90% of excess heat. The result: mass bleaching, acidification dissolving shell-building creatures, and rising seas reshaping coastlines.

30% increase in acidity
