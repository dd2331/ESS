Our oceans cover 70% of the planet, regulate our climate, and provide food for billions. But they are under threat. This project explores why ocean sustainability is non-negotiable.

Core Challenges in Ocean Sustainability

🚫 Overfishing
Global fish stocks are being depleted faster than they can recover. This disrupts entire food webs and threatens food security for coastal communities.
🧪 Pollution
From plastic gyres to chemical runoff, our oceans have become a global dumping ground. Microplastics are now found in virtually all marine life.
🌡️ Climate Change
The ocean absorbs 90% of the Earth's excess heat. This causes ocean warming, coral bleaching, and ocean acidification, which weakens shell-forming organisms.
