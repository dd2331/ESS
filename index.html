Core Threats

The Crisis,
Laid Bare

🚫
Overfishing

Global fish stocks are being stripped faster than they can ever recover. Entire food webs collapse as keystone species vanish, threatening the food security of over 600 million people who depend on fisheries.

⚠ 1 in 3 fish stocks — overexploited
🧪
Pollution

From the Great Pacific Garbage Patch to invisible microplastics, human waste has colonized every corner of the ocean. Microplastics now infiltrate the tissue of virtually every marine creature on Earth.

⚠ 51 trillion microplastic particles in our oceans
🌡️
Climate Change

The ocean absorbs 90% of the planet's excess heat. The result: mass coral bleaching events, rising sea levels, and ocean acidification that dissolves the shells of the creatures at the base of the food chain.

⚠ Ocean pH dropped 0.1 units — a 30% increase in acidity

What You Can Do

Small Actions,
Ocean Impact

01
Cut Single-Use Plastic

Swap plastic bags, straws, and bottles for reusable alternatives. It's one of the fastest wins for marine life.

02
Eat Sustainably

Choose seafood certified by the Marine Stewardship Council. Demand drives what fisheries supply.

03
Reduce Your Carbon

Every ton of CO₂ avoided helps slow ocean warming and acidification. Walk, cycle, offset.

04
Support Ocean NGOs

Organizations like Oceana and Ocean Conservancy fight daily for policy change and clean-up efforts.
